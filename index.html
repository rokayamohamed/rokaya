Graphic Design

Design is not merely an activity; it's a lifestyle. Consequently, I consistently incorporate design thinking into my work, allowing it to convey thoughts and ideas that words alone cannot express.

Story-telling

The human race processes thoughts using metaphors and acquires knowledge through narratives, which is why I am actively engaged in this educational expedition through the articles I contribute to my blog or the "Free Press School Newspaper."

Voice-overing

In live-action, the process unfolds scene by scene, whereas in voice-over work, it unfolds line by line. Voice-over work often involves multiple takes. To excel in voice-over, one must fully embrace a sense of freedom. It's not just about delivering lines but immersing oneself in the joy of make-believe. This method offers me a convenient avenue to engage with people's thoughts and foster empathy by stepping into their shoes through dubbing, enabling me to become a more attentive and empathetic listener.
